Act II · The free-bus debate

Free buses didn't break BMTC's finances.

The Shakti scheme gave women free travel — and got blamed for losses. The data disagrees: net losses are the lowest since COVID.

FY22-23 · pre-Shakti40%
FY23-24 · Shakti16%
FY24-25 · Shakti14%

Government reimbursement as a share of BMTC's costs — it fell from 40% to 14%. Free travel didn't sink the finances.

THE HONEST QUESTION
Not "free vs paid" — but who funds the subsidy, and is service investment protected?

The real strains are a Jan-2025 fare hike on other riders and chronic under-investment. The fix: fund concessions from the state budget as a transparent line item, ring-fence a dedicated transport fund, and grow non-fare revenue (land value capture / TOD).

Low-capex moves within ~12 months — operations, road-space reallocation, integration.

BUS

Bus-priority lanes, 10 worst corridors

Paint-and-bollard priority on ORR, Whitefield, Hosur Rd — the cheapest way to speed 44 lakh daily bus trips.

SYSTEM

Integrate BMTC + metro ticketing so a bus-to-metro trip is seamless, not double-charged.

SYSTEM
